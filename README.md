# Money Engine · 货币引擎

**The Nature of Money, Digital Currency, and the Future of Civilization.**
From barter to Bitcoin. From gold to algorithms. From national currencies to digital civilization.

A massive, bilingual (EN / 中文) single-page exploration of money, value, trust, credit, banking, central banks, Bitcoin, Ethereum, stablecoins, DeFi, privacy, CBDCs, tokenization, AI economies, Web3, UBI, and the future of money as planetary infrastructure — across 18 parts, each with its own interactive visualization.

## Stack

Plain HTML / CSS / vanilla JS — a single `index.html` with canvas/SVG visualizations (money-multiplier cascade, Bitcoin supply curve, AMM swap, peg-stability sim, zero-knowledge proof, DAO governance, planetary settlement network, and more), an instant EN/中文 toggle, animated value-stream hero, contents overlay, section rail, and scroll-reveal. No build step.
